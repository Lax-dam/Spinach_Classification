About Us Page

Passionate Developers

Welcome to our Spinach Classification and Prediction website! We are a team of passionate individuals who aim to provide a user-friendly tool for spinach leaf classification and identification. Our website allows users to upload images of spinach leaves and predicts their common name, scientific name, English name, and health benefits. Our goal is to make this information easily accessible to everyone and promote the importance of spinach in our daily lives. Thank you for visiting our website!
